Frequently Asked Questions

Why is learning digital skills important today?

Digital skills give people the ability to work, create, and earn from anywhere in the world. In a time where traditional opportunities are limited, learning digital skills opens doors to remote jobs, online businesses, and long-term personal growth.

Who is this platform meant for?

This platform is for beginners, students, and anyone willing to learn practical digital skills. It is especially designed for people who lack access to quality guidance, resources, or job opportunities, but are ready to improve their future through learning.

Is the learning free?

Yes. Most of the learning resources shared here are free or low-cost. The goal is to help people start learning even when finances are limited, and to prove that lack of money should not stop personal growth.

What is your main goal?

The main goal of the platform is to empower people with practical and useful skills. We aim to help learners become independent, confident, and capable of creating opportunities for themselves and others through knowledge and continuous learning.

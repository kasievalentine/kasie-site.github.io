Introduction to Digital Skills

Digital skills are abilities to effectively use technology, tools, and platforms. These skills are essential for freelancing, remote work, e-commerce, and online marketing.

Key Digital Skills to Learn

  • Graphic Design – Use Canva, Adobe Photoshop, or Figma.
  • Digital Marketing – Learn SEO, social media, email marketing.
  • Data Analysis – Use Excel, Google Analytics, or Tableau.
  • Programming & Web Development – HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python.

Where to Learn

Start developing digital skills on platforms like Coursera, Udemy, edX, or FreeCodeCamp.

Benefits

  • Access global freelance and remote work opportunities.
  • Start online businesses or e-commerce platforms.
  • Combine skills for multiple income streams like affiliate marketing and online jobs.
